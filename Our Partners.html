Pharmaccess Foundation

Poorer households bear the greatest burden of catastrophic health expenditures and low-quality healthcare. PharmAccess believes it can be done differently.

Ilara

Ilara Health brings accurate and affordable diagnostics to rural Africans through miniature, AI-powered diagnostic devices that are integrated via a proprietary technology platform and distributed directly to primary care doctors.

Hope WordWide

HOPE worldwide is an international charity that changes lives by harnessing the compassion and commitment of dedicated staff and volunteers to deliver sustainable, high-impact, community-based services to the poor and needy.

Linda Mama

Linda Mama is a programme targeting expectant Kenyan mothers who cannot afford any medical cover including NHIF to cater for their maternity requirements. Under this cover, mothers are covered for; pregnancy care, delivery in hospital, post-delivery care and outpatient baby care.

Triggerize - Tiko

Triggerise is a tech-powered, Netherlands-based non-profit operational in 10 countries in Sub-Saharan Africa and India. We motivate our users - primarily young women and adolescent girls - to make positive health choices.

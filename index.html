Our Mission

To develop and implement successful solutions to provide top quality and affordable healthcare to the people of Nairobi and Kenya at large and concentrate all our activities on the need of our patient. Strive for excellence in a unique brand of patient care delivered by people who are passionate about life, personal respect and dignity.

Our Vision

Delivering quality healthcare solutions to patients from all over Kenya. Successfully broadening access to affordable and sustainable quality healthcare to as many Kenyans as possible.

Our Pledge

  1. Enhance the experience and health outcomes with people accessing our services that are safe, effective, patient-centred, timely, efficient and equitable.
  2. Improve the health of our population
  3. Better use of resources for the benefit of our communities
  4. Build capability across our organisation and shared communities of practice.

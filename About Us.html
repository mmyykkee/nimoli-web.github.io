Nimoli Medicals Services Limited is registered in Kenya under the Act 486 and its offices are domiciled in Kenya. Nimoli is registered to carry on the business of running Health Clinics. Hospital, Medical Laboratories and Public Health utilities with all Medicines and Medical Equipments. To carry out Research and Development and to enter into partnership with Institutions/Organizations whether Governmental or Non Governmental in the field of Artificial Organs and regenerative Medical Care and to deal and distribute market specialized Medical Care Devices. The Company also carries out the business as general supliers of Medical Equipment, Surgical and Laboratory Equipments such as X-Ray, Magnetic Resonance Imaging Equipments and to franchise with other companies offering similar services and also to acquire whether by purchases, lease, exchange or otherwise offices, premises and accomadation for the purposes of leasing the same or making the same available to any person. firm or company. It has a Licence to operate as a Private Medical Institution under Medical and Practitioners and Dentists Act through Dr. Nicholas M. Muraguri, OGVV, MBChB, MPH, MBA. Nimoli has branches located in Kasarani and Tassia (Near to Tassia Hill Academy) Nairobi.

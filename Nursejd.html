Nurse Job Description

Position Summary:

The prime responsibility is to protect, promote and safeguard the well-being of all the clients/family and community in a professional and scientific manner; Responsibilities and Tasks Description:

• To provide nursing care to all patients in the facility
• Welcome patients to the consultation room for consultation, conduct required examinations on patients and update patient data using our electronic medical records systems
• Provide friendly, helpful service, guidance and education to all patients
• Delivering Child Welfare Clinic and Antenatal Clinic Services and administering Family planning services
• Conducting normal deliveries, diagnose and treat as appropriate any antenatal, labor and postnatal cases or complications
• Refer patients to the laboratory for tests if need for the same
• Monitoring drug charts and pharmacy room temperature charting
• Protect patients and other employees by adhering to infection-control policies and protocols
• Ensure drugs are stored appropriately and securely
• Prepares patient medical reports
• Undertake minor surgical operations
• Maintain pharmacy inventory by checking pharmaceutical stock to determine inventory level; anticipating needed medications and supplies; placing and expediting orders; verifying receipt; removing expired drugs;
• Ensuring and maintaining a safe and clean working environment that complies with procedures, rules, and regulations of Pharmacy and Poisons board
• Organizes dispensing of medication by reading medication orders and prescriptions; preparing labels; calculating quantities; assembling intravenous solutions and other pharmaceutical therapies
• Refer patients to other medical facilities if need be for further medication
• Schedules patients for revisits to monitor progress or response to prescribed drugs
• Recommend sick leave to working patients where necessary
• Hand over duty to the reliever staff
• Work closely with hospital management team and teammates to identify and implement opportunities to improve clinical quality and clinical operations
• Supervising cleanliness, waste management and linen quality
• Responsible for organization and developing standard operating procedures
• Maintaining the good working condition of Facility equipments
• Carry out other duties as assigned by the officer in-charge

Qualification Requirements:
Academic/Education:
• Diploma or equivalent from a recognized education institution
• Be a registered KRCHN by the Nursing council of Kenya
• Experience of a minimum of 2 years working in a busy hospital of clinic setting including experience handling complications during normal delivery
• Computer literate with knowledge of Microsoft Word, Excel and internet based systems
• Strong written and verbal skills in both English and local language;
• Excellent communication skills, demonstrated diplomacy and ability to elicit and foster positive relationships.
